Sap Calculator 3.5 is a comprehensive program for assessing heat loss in domestic buildings. The program is be used at the design stage to demonstrate compliance with the energy conservation requirements of Building Regulations Approved Document L1A 2010 and Technical Standards Section 6. The software is approved by the BRE for England, Wales and Scotland for the production of energy performance certificates. The Software was certified for use with the Scottish Domestic Register for use with BRE Scotland for the lodgement of EPC's on 30/10/13.

U-value Calculator

Version 3.5 includes a U-value calculator for calculations compliant with BRE 443, BS EN 6946, and ISO 13370. See menu on left for further details.
