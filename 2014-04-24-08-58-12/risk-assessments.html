Risk Assessments and Toolbox Talks

The software contains 68 default risk assessments and 70 Toolbox Talks within a powerful database, these can be easily duplicated, the assessment automatically includes all of the relevant project details. Each risk assessment can be customised where specific hazards are present in a project. The built-in PDF printer and contact list allows you to issue copies of the risk assessments individually to all relevant parties via e-mail with the correct information inserted into the assessment.

This is easily the most cost effective and productive risk assessment software tool available on the market today. Have a look at our tutorial to see how you can create, edit and distribute a risk assessments in under 5 minutes.

Key Benefits

  • 40 default risk assessment templates and 70 toolbox talks with buit-in mailmerge information of project details
  • Database system not MS Word documnents, you can duplicate and create your own templates. See the tutorials to find out how simple it is.
  • PDF Printer and email interface. No need for Adobe Acrobat.Issue assessments to multiple users via e-mail using a distribution list
  • Save PDF versions of the issued documents to your computer. No need for confusing system managing file names of various documents.
  • Database includes contact list to insert information into the templatesIncorporate your logo and signature automatically in the documents
